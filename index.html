Virtual Doctors

Virtual doctor visit allows for remote consultation with a physician, using video or phone call, convenient and accessible, can diagnose and treat common conditions, helps save time and reduce exposure, prescription medication can be delivered.

Diet

Include fruits, veggies, whole grains, lean protein, low-fat dairy, limit sugar/salt, drink plenty of water, avoid processed foods, limit alcohol, choose healthy fats.

Meditation

Meditation improves mental health, reduces stress and anxiety, enhances focus and concentration, promotes inner peace and happiness, and leads to better physical health.

Safety Tips

Wash hands regularly, avoid close contact, wear mask, cover cough/sneeze, avoid touching face, clean/disinfect frequently, stay home if sick, get vaccinated, exercise, eat well.
