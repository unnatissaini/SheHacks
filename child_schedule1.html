Newborn Child Daily Schedule and Routine

Time Activity
6am - 9am
  • Wake up and feed your baby
  • Burp your baby to release any trapped air
  • Change your baby's diaper if needed
  • Spend some time bonding with your baby through cuddling, talking, and singing
9am - 12pm
  • Put your baby down for a nap in a quiet, dark room
  • While your baby is napping, take some time for yourself to rest, eat, or take care of household chores
12pm - 3pm
  • Wake up your baby and feed them
  • Burp your baby to release any trapped air
  • Change your baby's diaper if needed
  • Spend some time playing with your baby, such as talking, singing, or reading books
3pm - 6pm
  • Put your baby down for a nap in a quiet, dark room
  • While your baby is napping, take some time for yourself to rest, eat, or take care of household chores
6pm - 9pm
  • Feed your baby, either breast milk or formula
  • Change your baby's diaper
  • Interact and play with your baby to help them stay awake a little longer before bed
  • Gently rock or cuddle your baby to help them relax
9pm - 12am
  • Put your baby down for a longer sleep, usually the longest stretch of sleep your baby will have during the night
  • Make sure your baby is in a safe sleeping position, such as on their back
  • Take some time for yourself to rest or sleep
12am - 3am
  • Wake up your baby and feed them
  • Burp your baby to release any trapped air
  • Change your baby's diaper if needed
  • Put your baby back to sleep
3am - 6am
  • Put your baby down for another nap in a quiet, dark room
  • While your baby is napping, take some time for yourself to rest, eat, or take care of household chores

Remember, every baby is different and may have different sleep and feeding patterns. Adjust this schedule to fit your baby's needs and consult with a pediatrician if you have any concerns.
